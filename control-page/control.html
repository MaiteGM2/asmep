Recommendations for SMD Soldering

PREHEAT N°1

The temperature should be in the range 150 - 180 to avoid thermal shock.

PREHEAT N°2

Stabilize at 180-200°C for moisture removal before reflow.

PEAK TEMPERATURE

230-250°C to melt solder without damaging components.

COOLING

Cooling should gradually reduce the temperature to solidify the solder at a rate of 3-5°C per second.
